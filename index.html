About Me

I am a Mathematics and Computer Science student at Saint Petersburg State University, with a strong focus on algebraic disciplines including K-theory, homotopy theory, and algebraic geometry.

My research explores the intersections of algebra, topology, and number theory, with particular interest in homology stability for linear groups, torsion groups of elliptic curves, and combinatorial number theory.

Beyond research, I am passionate about mathematical education and outreach, having organized seminars and taught at various mathematical schools.
